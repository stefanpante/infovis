Formula One Visualisation

Hi there! We are Giuseppe, Stefan and Michael, three students Computer Science at the University of Leuven.
We prouldy present to you an interactive visualisation in which you can analyze the performances
of your favourite drivers and teams throughout the F1-History

Check out our visualisation:
stefanpante.github.io/infovis/

or read more about its features below

Bar charts/ year

Compare the performance of two drivers by studying the stacked bar charts. These show the number of wins of a driver, his team member and thus the whole team performance, for each year.

Slider

Navigate through the careers of drivers by dragging the slider left and right.

Statistics

Compare the amount of first, second and third places a driver finished in a year. The numbers on the left and right top indicate the ranking of the corresponding driver.

Substitute

First select a new driver by clicking on a bar. Then indicate which driver will be replaced by the new one.

Line charts

The line charts show the evolution of a driver's career.

Team scores

If a team has scored a total of zero point, a small bar is still drawn in order to show the user that the team has competed with in this season.
